Smash Hit Lab Website

The Smash Hit Lab Website — which is powered by Labbyware, a custom content system — is a combined news, forum, wiki and mods database website for the game Smash Hit, a game which I have been very involved with.

Future plans

In the future, I would like to expand on the idea of making "community creation software." This kind of thing doesn't really exist today. Think of an exprience similar to Fandom, but better integrated becuase it isn't community platform slapped on top of MediaWiki. It has tools needed to form a community, like a place for discussions, a wiki for keeping information, a blog or news section for staff, messages and maybe more in one place.
