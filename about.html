Where does the name "Knot" come from?

I was signing up for Fandom, and I wanted a new username. As a result, I randomly chose the word "knot" and number 126 to make my new username "knot126". I ended up liking the name, so it stuck, and it's now what I identify as with my friends.

The word "knot" refers to a knot that one would tie with a rope. Not anything else.
